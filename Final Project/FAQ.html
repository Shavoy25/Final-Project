Frequently Asked Questions

How much do piñatas cost?

Our ready-made piñatas range from $20 to $50 depending on size and design. Custom orders start at $35 and increase based on complexity and materials.

How long does it take to make a custom piñata?

Custom orders typically take 5–7 business days to complete. During holidays or high-demand periods, please allow up to 10 days.

Do you deliver piñatas?

Yes! We offer local delivery in the Kingston, Jamaica area. Delivery fees may apply based on distance. Pickup from our location is also available and free of charge.

What materials are used in your piñatas?

All piñatas are crafted from recyclable paper, cardboard, and safe, non-toxic adhesives. Custom piñatas may include accessories depending on the request.

Can I request a piñata in a specific shape or character?

Absolutely! Just head to the Custom Orders page and fill out the form with your preferred shape, theme, colors, and any reference images you have.

What is your return policy?

Due to the handmade and customized nature of our products, all sales are final. However, if there’s an issue with your order, please contact us and we’ll do our best to help.
